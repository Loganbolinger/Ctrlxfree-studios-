About the Artist

I am a young man in my early 20s, chasing more than just a dream — chasing the freedom to live beyond the cage of reality. I’ve been drawing for as long as I can remember, shaping worlds where pop culture collides with the supernatural, where the everyday bends into the impossible.

My art is my voice, my protest, my invitation. I want to create not just for myself, but for everyone who feels trapped — to show that there are other worlds we can live in, if only we dare to imagine them.
